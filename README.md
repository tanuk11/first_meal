# 🥗 First Meal - Health & Wellness Platform

**First Meal** is a health-focused web application designed to promote a healthier lifestyle by providing users with valuable information about fitness, nutrition, and wellness. In addition to educational content, the platform also offers a curated collection of healthy products that users can explore and purchase.

🔗 **Live Demo:** https://first-meal.vercel.app/

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Maintaining a healthy lifestyle requires both knowledge and access to quality products. **First Meal** bridges this gap by offering:

* Informative content related to health and wellness.
* Guidance on adopting healthy habits.
* A marketplace featuring healthy products that support users in their fitness journey.

The goal of this project is to encourage individuals to make informed choices about their health while providing convenient access to products that align with their wellness goals.
